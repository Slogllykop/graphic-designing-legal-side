What are Some Common Legal Issues Associated with Graphic Designers?

There are various laws and statutes that govern and apply to graphic design. Due to its connection with advertising and marketing, graphic design sometimes involves legal issues such as:

  • 1] Disputes over trademarks, copyrights, and other protected intellectual property
  • 2] Breach of business contracts (many graphic designers work under contract rather than direct employment)
  • 3] Use of deception or unethical business advertising practices in a design
